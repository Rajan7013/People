# Employee Login Guide

## For New Employees

When you are added to the PeopleOS system, you will receive login credentials to access your employee portal.

### Login Process:

1. **Visit the PeopleOS Portal**: http://localhost:3000
2. **Use your credentials**:
   - **Email**: The email address provided during your employee setup
   - **Temporary Password**: A secure temporary password will be provided to you

### Default Login Credentials:

- **Email**: Your work email address
- **Temporary Password**: Generated automatically (will be provided by HR/Admin)

### First Time Login:

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter the temporary password provided
4. You will be prompted to change your password for security
5. Choose a strong password that meets these requirements:
   - At least 8 characters long
   - Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
   - Include numbers
   - Include special characters

### Forgot Password:

If you forget your password:
1. Contact your HR department or system administrator
2. They can reset your password and provide new temporary credentials

### Security Tips:

- Never share your login credentials
- Log out when you're done using the system
- Use a strong, unique password
- Report any suspicious activity to your IT department

### Need Help?

Contact your HR department or IT support for assistance with login issues.
